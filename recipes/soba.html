Sanji's Soba

Description

The recipe for Sanji's special Soba fom One Piece.

Ingredients

  • 2 serving soba noodle

Soba Soup

  • 650ml dashi
  • 2tbsp soy sauce
  • 1/2 tbsp cane sugar
  • 1 tbsp sea salt

Toppings

  • 50g boiled spinach
  • 4 slices fish cake
  • 3 tbsp tempura bits

Tempura Bits

  • 2 tbsp flour
  • 2 and 1/2 tbsp water
  • 1/2 tbsp vinegar

Instructions

To make Soba Soup

  1. Add soy sauce, sugar and salt to the soup stock and heat over medium heat until it comes to a light simmer.

To prepare toppings

  1. Squeeze out the water from the boiled spinach and cut into 5cm / 0.96 inch lengths.
  2. Cut the fish cake into 1cm pieces.
  3. Make tempura bits. In a small bowl, mix flour, water, and vinegar well.
  4. After heating your favorite vegetable oil to 170 degrees, use a brush, chopsticks, or your hands to drop the dough into the oil so that it forms small balls.
  5. When they are crispy, scoop them up with a spoon and drain the oil on paper towel.

To boil Soba

  1. Boil a large pot of water (2liters / 86oz) for boiling the soba noodles. Also, heat 1 liter (34oz) of water in a separate pot. Put soba noodles into 2 liters of hot water and stir well.
  2. If the buckwheat flour content is high, the water level will gradually rise along with the bubbles. If you add a small amount of water or adjust the heat, it will not overflow, so don't be panic!!
  3. Boil according to the directions on the package. After boiling, rinse off the wetness from the buckwheat noodles under running water.
  4. Put the noodles back into the hot water that has been heated separately.

To serve

  1. Drain the water from the soba in a strainer and serve it in a donburi bowl.
  2. Pour soup over noodles and top with spinach, fish cake, and tempura bits.
